only one auto popover can be displayed at a time

adding popover="auto" (or just default "popover" without value) tell browser that you expect automatic behaviour of light-dismissed popovers Therefore you can't open two popovers at the same time, because clicking one button to open will mean closing any other popovers that are open
<%inject only-one-at-a-time.htm %>

popover="manual" popovers can be displayed at once, bt they cant be light-dismissed

adding popover="manual" tell browser that you will take control over opening and closing - (for example clicking on the background will not close popup - light-dismissed)
<%inject multiple-can-be-displayed.htm %>

With close button

defining regular just `popover` without value (which will be `auto` by default) means you expect light-dismissed popovers. But clicking in the popover won't auto-close it. so you can introduce special element with attr [popovertargetaction="hide"] that will close the popover even from within the popover
