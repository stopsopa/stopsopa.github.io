Camel case and others

  • Camel Case:
    camelSnakeCaseVariable
    A combination of camel case and snake case, where words are both separated by underscores and the initial letter of each word is capitalized.
  • Pascal Case (or Upper Camel Case):
    PascalCaseIdentifier
  • Snake Case:
    snake_case_variable
  • Kebab Case:
    kebab-case
  • Upper Kebab Case:
    UPPER-KEBAB-CASE
  • Screaming Snake Case (or SCREAMING_SNAKE_CASE):
    SCREAMING_SNAKE_CASE_CONSTANT
    Often used for constants.
  • Hungarian Notation:
    strFirstName, intCounter
    In Hungarian notation, a variable name starts with a prefix that indicates the variable's type.
  • Train Case (or Title Case):
    Train-Case-Heading
    Similar to kebab case, but with the first letter of each word capitalized. Often used for headings or titles.
  • Macro Case:
    MACRO_CASE
    Similar to SCREAMING SNAKE CASE, often used for macros in languages like C/C++.
